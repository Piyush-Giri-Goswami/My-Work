Delta Pro

The Delta Pro is a high-capacity portable power station by EcoFlow, designed for both home backup and outdoor use, providing up to 3600Wh of power.

Key Features

  • Massive Power Capacity

    3,600Wh, expandable up to 25,000Wh with additional batteries.

  • Fast Charging

    Fully charges in under 2.5 hours with AC or 1 hour using X-Stream technology.

  • Multiple Outputs

    AC outlets, USB ports, and car socket for various devices.

  • Portability

    Lightweight and equipped with wheels and an ergonomic handle.

  • Smart Features

    Mobile app for remote monitoring and control.

  • Sustainable Power Options

    Solar recharging for an eco-friendly solution.

EcoFlow Delta Pro is a versatile portable power station, ideal for outdoor enthusiasts, RV users, and as an emergency backup power solution.
